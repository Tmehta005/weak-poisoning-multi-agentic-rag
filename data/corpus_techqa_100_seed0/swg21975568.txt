ICN; Content Navigator; Save; Save and Close; Button
Technote (troubleshooting)
Problem(Abstract)
In IBM Content Navigator, when configuring a desktop, the 'Save and Close' and 'Save' buttons may be disabled.
Cause
One of the required fields in the configuration does not have a value
Resolving the problem
IBM Content Navigator configuration tabs that contain required fields are flagged with a red asterisk to the side of the tab label. For a desktop configuration, General, Repositories, Layout, and Menus tabs contain required fields. Make sure all these fields have a value.
